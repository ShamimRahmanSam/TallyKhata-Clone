Business accounting is very easy

Keep accounts in Talikhata app without using different accounts for balance, cash, payments. The pie-to-pie calculation of the business will be in front of the eyes.

  • Very easy to record transactions

Transaction message improves customer relationship

Users send messages to customers and suppliers in case of credit transactions. It removes misunderstanding among them regarding payable and receivable amounts. Hence relationship among them improves, consequently sales increase.

  • Send details of sold products to customers
